Recipe - Chicken Tinga

Chicken Tinga (Serves 4-6)

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shredded cooked chicken
  • 3 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Corn tortillas (optional, for serving)
  • Optional toppings: avocado, sour cream, cheese

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Saut√© onions until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Blend tomatoes, chipotle peppers, and chicken broth in a blender until smooth.
  3. Pour the blended mixture into the pan with onions and garlic. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add shredded chicken to the sauce, mixing well. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes to let the flavors combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with warm tortillas and your favorite toppings. Enjoy!
