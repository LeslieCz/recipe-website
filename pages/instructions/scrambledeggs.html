Recipe - Scrambled Eggs

Scrambled Eggs (Serves 2-4)

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk (optional)
  • 2 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, chopped herbs, or diced vegetables

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add milk (if using), a pinch of salt, and pepper. Whisk until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or olive oil.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet and let it sit for a few seconds until the edges start to set.
  4. Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs, pushing them from the edges toward the center to form soft curds.
  5. Cook to your desired consistency, stirring occasionally (avoid overcooking for creamier eggs).
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with your favorite toppings like cheese, herbs, or veggies. Serve immediately and enjoy!
