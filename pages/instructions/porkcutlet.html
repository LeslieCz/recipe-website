Recipe - Air-Fried Pork Cutlet with Rice

Air-Fried Pork Cutlet with Rice (Serves 2-4)

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 3 cups water
  • Optional garnish: sliced green onions or sesame seeds
