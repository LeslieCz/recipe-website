Recipe - Air-Fried Pork Cutlet with Rice

Air-Fried Pork Cutlet with Rice (Serves 2-4)

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless pork chops
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking spray
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 3 cups water
  • Optional garnish: sliced green onions or sesame seeds

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your air fryer to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season pork chops with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Set up a breading station with flour, beaten eggs, and panko breadcrumbs in separate bowls.
  4. Coat each pork chop in flour, then dip into the eggs, and finally coat with panko breadcrumbs. Ensure even coverage.
  5. Lightly spray both sides of the breaded pork chops with cooking spray and place them in the air fryer basket. Cook for 12-15 minutes, flipping halfway, until golden brown and cooked through (internal temperature of 145°F).
  6. While the pork cooks, rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Add rice and water to a rice cooker and start the cooking cycle.
  7. Once the rice and pork are ready, serve the pork cutlet over a bed of rice. Garnish with sliced green onions or sesame seeds if desired.
  8. Enjoy your crispy, air-fried pork cutlet with fluffy rice!
