Recipe - Classic Chili

Classic Chili (Serves 6-8)

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef or turkey
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 2 cups beef or vegetable broth
  • 1 (15 oz) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15 oz) can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (optional, for heat)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add chopped onion, green bell pepper, and garlic. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until softened.
  2. Add ground beef or turkey to the pot, cooking until browned and fully cooked. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in chili powder, cumin, paprika, and cayenne pepper (if using). Cook for 1 minute to bring out the flavors of the spices.
  4. Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and beef broth to the pot. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir in kidney beans and black beans. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover, and let the chili simmer for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve with your favorite toppings like sour cream, shredded cheese, green onions, or tortilla chips. Enjoy!
