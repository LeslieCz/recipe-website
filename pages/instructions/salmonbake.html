Recipe - Salmon Bake

Sushi Bake (Serves 3-5)

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb salmon, seasoned to taste
  • 1/2 lb imitation crab, shredded
  • 3 oz cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup Japanese mayo
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 2-3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • Furikake or shredded seaweed (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Season salmon (garlic powder, salt, pepper) and air fry at 400°F for 9 minutes. Shred the salmon and mix with imitation crab.
  2. Add cream cheese, mayo, and sriracha to the salmon mixture. Set aside.
  3. Mix cooked rice with rice vinegar and spread evenly in an oven-safe dish.
  4. Top rice with furikake or shredded seaweed, then spread the salmon mixture over it.
  5. Bake at 380°F for 10 minutes (broil for 2-3 minutes if desired).
  6. Serve with seaweed wraps, cucumbers, or avocado. Enjoy!
